Ever been asked how you feel from a scale from 1 to 10? The World Happiness Report is a publication that is published each year that asks almost everyone in the world just that! This page will focus on the latest report in 2021 including the factors that may affect how happy a country is. These include GDP per capita, social support, life expectancy, perception of freedom, generosity and perception of corruption.

Happiest Country - Finland

Finland was the happiest country in the World Happiness Report 2021. Scandinavian countries have always been among the highest, and experts have determined that this is due to many factors. Apart from the factors listed in this report, Scandinavian countries also have free education, healthcare, a well-functioning democracy and a high priority on life balance.
Unhappiest Country - Afghanistan

Afghanistan was unfortunately found to be the unhappiest country in the World Happiness Report 2021. This is very largely due to the political turmoil that Afghanistan has been going through in the past couple of decades. This was particulary augmented by the withdrawal of US troops, leaving Afghanistan to change in leadership, much to some people's dismay.
